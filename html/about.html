About Us

VISION

Constructed from the tenants of sustainability, conservation, and respect for all life, we strive to create an immersive experience that gives the individual a look into the aquatic world and leave with a connection that drives him or her to personally engage in protecting our planet.

Our Mission

Compassion

Compassion for animals evokes empathy for all of nature, which is marvelous, robust and equally fragile.

Curiosity

Curiosity highlights how the Finley experience brings people closer to animals and their ecosystems.

Conservation

Conservation underscores the importance of protecting wild species and wild spaces in an urbanizing world.

Our Values

At the Finley Aquatic, our core values form the basis for our beliefs about our organization, our colleagues and ourselves, and serve as the framework to guide our behavior and actions to achieve our mission: to inspire conservation of the world's aquatic treasures.

Be true. Build trust. Do right. We do the right thing and accept responsibility for that which is ours.

Be original. Challenge norms. Take chances. We support well-conceived risk taking, embrace creativity and consistently pursue new opportunities.
