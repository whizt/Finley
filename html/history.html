History

HISTORY

As the world changes and technology advances, there has been a lot of harm done by humans to mother nature itself. One of those natures being the ocean. Finley Corporation was founded by two fisherman who one day came to realization of how the ocean has been affected by humans. A lot of wild life destroid of their habitats and water pollution being spread around the ocean are some of the view problems at hand. Therefore at the year 1981 Finley Corporation was born.

Our Story

Finley Corporation opened on August 8, 1981. With a mission to inspire conservation of the world's aquatic treasures, featuring thousands of fishes living in. We prioritize our work to focus on saving wildlife and habitats, and stopping plastic pollution. We have rescued, rehabilitated and released hundreds of endangered sea turtles throughout the mid-Atlantic region. Through conservation action and advocacy, the Finley Corporation is pursuing a vision to change the way humanity cares for our ocean planet.
